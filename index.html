About us

No Barriers Communicate Easier

Over 6.3% of the Indian population is suffering from Significant Auditory Impairments. They communicate their messages and needs with the help of Indian Sign Language(ISL). ISL serves as a communication tool and a mark of the hearing-impaired community's pride and identity. As a result, it is critical that people recognize its significance. Sign Companion App was created with the goal of accurately translating speech, text, or documents into Indian Sign Language Animations. It is important to us that the sign's original meaning is not distorted and that people are able to communicate with the hearing impaired with ease, reducing communication gaps. The app was created by a team of developers passionate about making a difference in the lives of those with hearing impairments. The app not only provides a platform for people to connect with the hearing impaired via ISL Animations but also affords users the opportunity to learn ISL through the “Learn-ISL” feature quickly.
